Axiusflow is not trying to clone every TradingView feature. It is built for traders who want live crypto charting, a trading journal, trade replay, AI-powered reviews, imports, and analytics in one review workflow.

Live crypto charting is available now. Trade history can be imported for journaling and replay, and additional broker, futures, equities, forex, and options workflows are being added as data licensing and integrations become commercially viable.

Trading data should be treated as sensitive by default. Axiusflow is designed around encrypted storage, scoped broker access where supported, and privacy-first workflows so your trading records remain under your control.
